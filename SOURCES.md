## Key Capabilities for Hotels

### What ElevenLabs ConvAI Can Do for Hospitality
1. **24/7 Virtual Concierge** — Check-in, room info, local recommendations, billing
2. **Reservation Management** — Availability checks, changes, cancellations, special requests
3. **Multilingual Support** — 31 languages with native-sounding speech
4. **Restaurant & Dining** — Table bookings, menu queries, dietary restrictions, POS integration
5. **Upselling** — Dynamic prompts with real-time context (booking details, preferences, loyalty)
6. **Sentiment Detection** — Identifies dissatisfied guests, offers remediation, escalates high-risk
7. **Omnichannel** — Voice, SMS, web chat from a single AI receptionist
8. **PMS/POS Integration** — Works with Aircall, Genesys, Salesforce, Zendesk, Stripe, custom APIs

### Business Impact (from ElevenLabs case studies)
- One luxury travel client: **$300K+ revenue in 90 days** from conversational booking assistant
- **58%** of guests believe AI improves their booking and stay
- **30%** higher satisfaction vs. rule-based bots
- **65%** of guests expect hotel tech to outpace their own devices
